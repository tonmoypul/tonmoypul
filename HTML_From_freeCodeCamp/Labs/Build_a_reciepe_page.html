Egg Curry

Some thing...

The most commonly eaten food by the bachelor students of university is Egg Curry. It's favorite to them because of its easy to make recipe and the short time it takes.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Potato
  • Onion and other spices

Instructions

  1. First cut the onions into small pieces ( at least two regular sized onion for one egg).
  2. Boil the eggs and potatoes and mash the potatoes when they are boiled properly. Fry the boiled eggs at a minimum heat.
  3. Fry the onions and give the mashed potatoes on the pan. Marinade them with other necessary spicery.
  4. Then pour some water into the pan and put the eggs on the water and boil them properly. And you've got your Egg Curry.
